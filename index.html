Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of mixed vegetables (potatoes, cauliflower, peas, carrots)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2-3 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 teaspoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 4-6 pav buns

Instructions:

  1. Boil the mixed vegetables until tender, then mash them with a potato masher.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a pan and sauté the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic and ginger and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until they are soft and mushy.
  5. Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the mashed vegetables and mix well. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Toast the pav buns with butter.
  8. Serve the pav bhaji with the toasted pav buns and garnish with chopped onions and cilantro.
